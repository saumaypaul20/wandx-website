  • We are integrating decentralized KYC, decentralized credit scoring, social account recovery, fiat on-off ramps, instant payments, and enabling portfolio based investments and SIP based investments.

  • We provide a seamless user experience for users to simply deposit Bitcoin in an interest bearing account through enabling the Bitcoin to be “staked” on different Blockchains.

  • Payment using Bitcoin would happen using Bitcoin lightning and the PayID protocol.

  • Storing Bitcoin would happen on the device of the user. The “wallet” would be a part of the FIO protocol.

  • KYC would happen with decentralized KYC partners.
  • Taking credit would only happen once a credit score has been built using decentralized credit systems.
  • Credit can be given by any party in our system.
  • Every action on the app is through a decentralized platform.

We enable lending of Bitcoin for currencies, stocks, and other cryptocurrencies. We enable staking of Bitcoin for Tezos and other staking tokens.

We partner with fund managers, custodians, asset managers who take bitcoin for buying currencies and stocks

Create your Bitcoin account, invest your Bitcoin in a basket of cryptocurrencies built on the Ethereum Blockchain (ERC20 Tokens). These baskets are classified as: Basket of low market cap ERC20 Tokens, Top 5 ERC20 Tokens, Basket of Stable coins built on Ethereum. They are also classified by theme: Decentralized cloud storage, decentralized streaming tokens, decentralized identity tokens, decentralized gambling Tokens.

These baskets can be created by anyone. WandX does not create any of these baskets, but provides the means for people to be able to create these baskets through the WandX desktop application.
